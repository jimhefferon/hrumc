\abstract{% title
    Working the elliptic
  }{% authors
    Steve Jones, UBC
  }{% level
    1}{% abstract
  The elliptic is very hard to work with.  In this talk we emphasize
  the difficulty, including the difficulty of the mathematics: 
  $\int_0^1 x^2\,dx$ and the geometry $\sin(x^3)\neq (\sin(x))^3$.
}
